Rules

Brake them and we will destroy you

All raiders must download the Monolith DKP addon (https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/monolith-dkp)
in order to check their DKPs at all times and to be able to place bids on items that are of interest to them.
In the table below you can find all the values that can make raiders win or lose DKPs.
You should only go to the officials if you see a REAL problem with your DKPs and it is not happening in the rest of the raiders.
DON'T bother to ask how many DKPs you have.
DON'T bother to say that you are missing DKPs for food or elixir, just wait a bit and those DKPs will be assigned.
Any questions of this kind should be made after the raid is over.
The person of the guild in charge of the DKPs is the officer Agatio.
He assumes a role and responsibility to make everything work properly and the guild can enjoy a fair and equitable system in the distribution of items.
Please do not disturb him if it is not strictly necessary. Thank you.
